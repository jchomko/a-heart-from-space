A Heart from Space is a website that enables a group of people to draw together using their GPS positions.

Each participant holds a point of the drawing and moves with the group to create a heart.

When you click start, you'll be asked to share some sensor permissions - these allow the website to access your GPS position and compass sensor.

When you're happy with the shape of the heart, tap the heart button on the bottom right of the screen to fill your section. When the heart is full of colour, the heart is ready.
