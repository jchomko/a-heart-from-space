A Heart from Space is an experiment in collective creation, allowing a group of people to draw shapes together. Each person's GPS location creates one point on the line, allowing them to adjust one section of the drawing by moving in space.

The website will request access to your GPS location and compass, and will share this data with your group only; no data is stored. For the best results, a group of six to eight people is required, but smaller groups also work.

Start New Heart

To join a heart that someone has started, ask them to send you the link to their heart. Opening the link will connect you directly to their heart.
